# Card 01 - The Magician

## Role

This card reframes the leopard cat as an environmental strategist:
resourceful, adaptive, precise, and capable of survival in fragmented habitats.

## Visual Brief

- Setting: agricultural edge near field ridges and low vegetation
- Subject: an adult male leopard cat at a turning point in the field path
- Pose: partially concealed in grass, eyes sharp, forepaw moving a dry leaf

## Four-Element Mapping

- Wand: a dry reed stalk
- Cup: pooled water in a footprint depression
- Sword: a sharp blade of silver grass
- Pentacle: the visible opening of a rodent burrow

## Mood

- focused
- intelligent
- stealthy
- grounded in material survival

## Symbolic Layer

- tarot meaning: manifestation, focus, communication, skill
- ecological meaning: hunting technique, edge adaptation, habitat intelligence

## Image Notes

- the four elemental objects should feel native to the habitat, not staged props
- composition should imply mastery without losing the vulnerability of a fragmented environment
