# Card 02 - The High Priestess

## Role

This card deepens the deck into nocturnal intuition and hidden habitat knowledge.
It should feel like a portal into stillness, water, and undisturbed awareness.

## Visual Brief

- Setting: streamside rocks, mist, and deep forest margin
- Subject: a leopard cat seated on a smooth stone near moving water
- View: profile facing the water
- Reflection: not a literal mirror image, but a star-lit ancient forest

## Supporting Elements

- cold silver and deep blue palette
- a faintly glowing piece of decaying wood near the paws
- trees forming an arch-like structure that echoes the High Priestess pillars

## Mood

- quiet
- lunar
- sacred
- observant

## Symbolic Layer

- tarot meaning: intuition, silence, subconscious, inner knowing
- ecological meaning: dependence on clean water, hidden habitat, low-disturbance survival

## Image Notes

- avoid melodrama; the strength of the card should come from restraint
- water, mist, and reflected space should carry more weight than action
