# Card 00 - The Fool

## Role

The opening card establishes the emotional grammar of the deck:
wild innocence, fragile curiosity, and the danger of crossing human-made borders.

## Visual Brief

- Setting: high grass at the forest edge
- Left side: dense, shadowed native habitat
- Right side: a straight asphalt road extending into the unknown
- Subject: a 3-4 month old leopard cat stepping toward the roadside boundary
- Key detail: one forepaw suspended above the white line, signaling threshold and risk

## Mood

- innocent
- luminous
- uneasy
- tender but threatened

## Symbolic Layer

- tarot meaning: new beginnings, curiosity, trust, risk
- ecological meaning: juvenile dispersal and road mortality danger

## Image Notes

- emphasize the ear-back white spot as species identity
- keep the vehicle light distant enough to feel seductive, not yet fully catastrophic
- use deep forest green, twilight blue, and a small amount of warning amber/red
