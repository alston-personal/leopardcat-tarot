# Symbolic Mapping

## Core Method

Map each tarot card to:

1. a recognizable tarot archetype
2. a real leopard cat behavior, habitat condition, or conservation reality
3. a visual composition that can carry both meanings at once

## Major Arcana Anchors

- `The Fool`: juvenile dispersal, threshold, road danger
- `The Magician`: adaptive hunting, environmental intelligence, edge survival
- `The High Priestess`: moonlit water, hidden habitat, intuition through stillness

## Supporting Major Arcana Ideas

- `The Hermit`: hidden den, fog, isolation, inner wisdom
- `The Moon`: nocturnal uncertainty, reflected truth, forest shadow
- `Justice`: balance between human expansion and species survival
- `The Tower`: habitat destruction and forced displacement
- `The World`: restored ecological harmony

## Minor Arcana Translation

- Wands -> branches, reeds, thorny growth, territorial movement
- Cups -> water, stream systems, habitat nourishment
- Swords -> alertness, claws, sharp grass, survival precision
- Pentacles -> prey, land fertility, grounded material life
