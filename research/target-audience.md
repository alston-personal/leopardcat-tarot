# Target Audience

## Group 1 - Conservation Advocates

- motivated by species protection and biodiversity
- may buy the deck as a meaningful conservation object
- care about the ecological truth behind the imagery

## Group 2 - Tarot And Spiritual Collectors

- seek new symbolic systems beyond traditional Rider-Waite repetition
- value atmosphere, symbolism, and collectible uniqueness
- likely to appreciate the deck's local spiritual identity

## Group 3 - Art And Design Audiences

- may not use tarot in practice
- attracted to illustration quality and thematic originality
- likely to share visuals across social platforms

## Strategic Insight

The deck becomes strongest when one object satisfies all three groups:

- emotionally resonant
- visually collectible
- ecologically grounded
