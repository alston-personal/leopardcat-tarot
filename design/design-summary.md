# Design Summary

## Project Position

LeopardCat Tarot sits at the intersection of:

- ecological storytelling
- mystical symbolism
- collectible visual design

## Current Design Thesis

Each card should map one tarot archetype to one real ecological fragment from the life of the leopard cat.

This system gives the deck:

- symbolic coherence
- educational depth
- emotional distinctiveness

## Audience Strategy

Primary audience clusters:

- conservation advocates and ecological educators
- tarot readers and spiritual collectors
- visual culture and independent art audiences

## Balance Rule

The deck should avoid two failure modes:

- too scientific: becomes an illustrated ecology manual
- too mystical: loses conservation purpose and local specificity

## Product Direction

Preferred direction at this stage:

- serious, lyrical, visually refined
- emotionally deep rather than ornamental
- rooted in place, species, and real habitat pressure

## Next Design Decisions

- decide the dominant emotional tone
- define consistent card frame / typography / numbering system
- lock palette families for day, dusk, and nocturnal cards
- define how conservation text appears without crowding the illustration
