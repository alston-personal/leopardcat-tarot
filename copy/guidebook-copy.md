# Guidebook Copy

## Core Premise

LeopardCat Tarot is not only a divination deck.
It is a habitat diary, a conservation mirror, and a symbolic translation of Taiwan's leopard cat into tarot language.

## Reader Experience

When someone draws a card, they should encounter:

- a tarot archetype
- a specific emotional atmosphere
- a real ecological fragment
- a question about coexistence

## Voice Direction

- poetic but clear
- spiritually resonant without becoming vague
- conservation-aware without sounding like a textbook

## Deck Promise

This deck should help the reader feel that:

- intuition has a habitat
- wildness is intelligent, not ornamental
- every symbol has a cost in the real world

## Future Writing Slots

- introduction to the deck
- per-card upright / reversed interpretation
- conservation note for each card
- closing essay on habitat, destiny, and coexistence
